BOSS: the Bounded Open Safety Standard

Public overview · spec & kernel patent-pending

A governance standard for agentic AI, organized around a published governance directory: the machine-readable record a governed system registers to declare how it is governed.

"Unbounded agents are ungoverned agents."
what BOSS is

The governance surface a system exposes

BOSS specifies the governance surface a system must expose to make run-time reasoning load-bearing: the phase discipline, human checkpoints, execution contracts, knowledge model, and conformance, leaving transport, auth, and deployment to the implementation layer. It's modeled on how Kubernetes, OAuth, and OpenTelemetry commoditize a specification while value is captured at the implementation. The reference implementation is BOSNet.io. "Bounded" is a formal, checkable property: every part of BOSS bounds something.

the four guarantees

What must hold where reasoning does real work

Bounded

Inside the lines

Explicit policy, permissions, blast-radius limits. Prevents unauthorized action.

Evidence-backed

Provenance always

Every fact traces to hashed source evidence. Prevents hallucinated facts.

Audited

Tamper-evident

Every decision logs inputs, model, verdict. No "we can't say why."

Reversible

Recoverable

High-impact actions gated, versioned, recoverable. Contains a bad inference.

the centerpiece

The governance directory

A governed system must publish how it is governed. That machine-readable record is its governance directory: what an auditor, regulator, or partner reads to discover, verify, and audit the governance without access to the source. It enumerates seven things:

what a governed system publishes
1Policies & constraintsThe boundaries it operates under: what is admissible, and blast-radius limits on any action.
2Controls & gatesThe enforcement checkpoints and human-in-the-loop approvals that must be passed.
3Evidence sourcesThe hashed, provenance-tracked sources that back every asserted fact.
4Agent & tool rosterWhich agents/tools are registered and permitted. Omission is prohibition.
5Audit surfaceWhere the tamper-evident decision ledger lives and what each entry records.
6Reversibility guaranteesWhich actions are gated, versioned, recoverable, and how they roll back.
7Conformance claimThe BOSS conformance level asserted, and the evidence offered for it.
Discoverability over trust: governance that lives only in prose or intentions can't be verified. The directory turns "we govern our AI" into something an outside party can read and check, and every entry is itself an enforcement point.
conformance

A ramp across three levels

LEVEL 1

Foundation

Phase discipline + mandatory human checkpoints. A directory covering policies, a gate roster, an audit surface.

LEVEL 2

Structured

Adds deterministic, reproducible execution and machine-compiled governance, derived from a model rather than hand-written per feature.

LEVEL 3

Full

The complete surface: atomic evidence-backed knowledge, compiled governance, output-safety gates, checkable conformance.

Conformance is demonstrated: a system proves it against criteria and test vectors rather than self-certifying by checklist.

What this overview does not disclose

This publishes BOSS's principles, guarantees, directory structure, and conformance intent: enough to evaluate and adopt its shape. These remain proprietary and reserved:

  • the conformance rule set and test vectors;
  • the governance kernel / compiler, its dimensional model, schema, and coordinate scheme;
  • the reference implementation (BOSNet.io).
