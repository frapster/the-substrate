the conversion roadmap

Apply the proven pattern to the operational layer

The same envelope compiler + blueprints + auditor Relic Wars already ships, pointed at authoring and serving, not just ingestion. This is a plan; none of it has shipped.

#Step
1Blueprint the content typesDeclarative blueprints for what a lesson is, what a study article requires, what a valid publication looks like, replacing hand-coded page/lesson logic.
2Compile, don't branchA governed compiler turns a blueprint + retrieved evidence into a run-time plan. Curation, sequencing, and metadata become compiled reasoning.
3Auditor over authoringReject any artifact citing unretrieved sources or missing provenance: the Relic Wars commit-gate, applied to content.
4Raise the test floorBlueprint conformance + auditor checks become the regression net the current codebase (3 test files) lacks.
5Keep the transactional shellAuth, billing, RLS, and the publish gate stay deterministic code. frozen
